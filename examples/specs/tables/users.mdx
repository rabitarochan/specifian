## Design Notes

- A unique constraint is applied to email addresses to prevent duplicate registrations.
- Soft delete is not implemented; physical deletion is assumed.
- The `role` value should be validated at the application level to prevent invalid entries.
- Automatic timestamp management is recommended for `created_at` / `updated_at`.
